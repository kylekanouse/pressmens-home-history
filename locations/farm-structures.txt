Farm Structures: Barns and farmhouses sat several hundred yards to the west of Pressman’s Home, down the road from the administration and trade school buildings. The barn (pictured at right) was added in 1940.

The stables housed the horses used for recreational activities and later became hay storage before an arson fire in August of 2009 burned it to the ground. The Pressmen’s Home dairy barn later became the club house of the now defunct Camelot Golf & Country Club
